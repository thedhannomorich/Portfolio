Projects

Atlas

🔗 https://thedhannomorich.github.io/Atlas/

I built Atlas because I wanted an AI study assistant that actually understands how students learn. Unlike basic chatbots, Atlas supports image uploads and camera capture so you can snap a photo of a textbook page or a handwritten problem and get an instant answer. The app renders math equations beautifully using MathJax, streams responses in real time, lets you switch between different GPT models, includes text-to-speech, and has dark mode for late-night study marathons.
